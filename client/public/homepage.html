Frequently Asked Questions

What is Aspirely?

+

Aspirely is your personalized career companion. It helps you explore courses, colleges, and career paths that align with your goals and preferences.

How does the career quiz work?

+

The quiz gathers your interests, preferences, and academic background to suggest suitable career paths and entrance exams.

Is Aspirely free to use?

+

Yes, Aspirely is completely free. You can explore career options, colleges, and reports without any charges.

Can I retake the quiz or update my preferences?

+

Absolutely. You can retake the quiz at any time or edit your answers as your interests evolve.

Does Aspirely help with exam and college information too?

+

Yes. Aspirely provides guidance on entrance exams like CUET, JEE, NEET, CLAT, etc., and recommends colleges based on your chosen path and eligibility.
